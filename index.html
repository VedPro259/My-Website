Who am I?

Hello, my name is Ved Bhowmik, or you might have heard me from my producer alias name @vrodox.beats. I am a record producer, self-taught mixing engineer, software enthusiast, and music lover. Like every teenager during the quarantine, I too dwelled away at my desk listening to music, watching T.V., and putting up with online school. But, after too many days of listening to Kanye's discography and an abundant amount of experimental, soulful, jazz rap records on my playlist, I too wanted to experience how it felt making a beat. I began learning Garageband in 2020 and have gotten a lot better ever since. My first beat was an over distorted R&B sample topped with an off-beat drum pattern and no evident song structure. But, hey, everyone has to start somewhere, right? My sounds are constantly changing, but I work best with soulful, Lo-fi melodies, groovy rhythmic drum breaks, and an infectious warm bassline. Feel free to check out my beats and reach out.
