COMING 2024: PRESENTING TUNESVILLE
Most digital audio workstations are typically designed for experienced musicians looking to create professional sound recordings online. But who's to say that even children can't venture into the world of music-making? While most DAWs are open to everyone, it's undeniable that there's a lot of practice and frustration before you can craft your first tune. That's why I invented Tunesville. It's a DAW tailor-made for kids, allowing them to unleash their creativity without being bogged down by technicalities. Imagine this: a DAW equipped with ready-to-use audio clips that can be seamlessly dropped into your mix. Add in a variety of instruments, and voil√† – you've got a complete, harmonious track at your fingertips. But Tunesville isn't just software. It's a mentor, a friend that guides these young enthusiasts through the process of composing their very first song. From understanding beats per minute and rhythm to grasping the structure of a song and orchestrating different instruments, this companion covers it all. Once they've mastered the art, these budding musicians get the chance to proudly share their creations with others. Music production, simplified.
